About Me

Hello! I'm Aryatunga Dickens, a Medical Lab Technologist with a passion for web design. With over 10 years of experience in the medical field and a deep understanding of web development, I bring a unique perspective to both industries. My journey has been driven by a commitment to precision in lab diagnostics and creativity in web solutions.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Medical Laboratory Science and have completed various courses in web development. My work involves developing user-friendly web applications for healthcare professionals and patients, ensuring both functionality and aesthetic appeal.

My mission is to enhance healthcare through innovative technology, bridging the gap between medical science and modern web design.
